IN THE HIGH COURT OF CALCUTTA

Case Number: HC-CAL-0024-2026
Subject: Prevention of Sexual Harassment at Workplace related matters in Calcutta

JUDGMENT:
In Vishaka and others v State of Rajasthan (AIR 1997 SC 3011), the Supreme Court laid down guidelines (Vishaka Guidelines) to deal with sexual harassment of women at the workplace, which later influenced the POSH Act, 2013.
