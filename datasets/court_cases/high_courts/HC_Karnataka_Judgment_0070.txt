IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA

Case Number: HC-KAR-0070-2026
Subject: Decriminalization of Homosexuality related matters in Karnataka

JUDGMENT:
In Navtej Singh Johar & Ors. v. Union of India thr. Secretary Ministry of Law and Justice (W.P. (Crl.) No. 76 of 2016), the Supreme Court struck down Section 377 of the Indian Penal Code to the extent that it criminalized consensual sexual conduct between adults of the same sex.
