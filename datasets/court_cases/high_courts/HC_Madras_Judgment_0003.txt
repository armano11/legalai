IN THE HIGH COURT OF MADRAS

Case Number: HC-MAD-0003-2026
Subject: Right to Privacy related matters in Madras

JUDGMENT:
In Justice K. S. Puttaswamy (Retd.) and Anr. vs Union Of India And Ors. (Writ Petition (Civil) No. 494 of 2012), a nine-judge bench of the Supreme Court unanimously held that the right to privacy is a fundamental right protected under Article 21 of the Constitution.
