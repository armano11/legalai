IN THE SUPREME COURT OF INDIA

Case Number: SC-0026-2026
Title: Kesavananda Bharati v State of Kerala
Subject: Basic Structure Doctrine

JUDGMENT:
In Kesavananda Bharati Sripadagalvaru & Ors. v. State of Kerala & Anr. (Writ Petition (Civil) 135 of 1970), the Supreme Court of India outlined the Basic Structure doctrine of the Constitution. The Court held that Parliament cannot alter the basic structure of the Constitution under Article 368.
