IN THE SUPREME COURT OF INDIA

Case Number: SC-0147-2026
Title: Maneka Gandhi v Union of India
Subject: Right to Life and Personal Liberty

JUDGMENT:
In Maneka Gandhi v. Union of India (1978 AIR 597), the Supreme Court expanded the scope of Article 21, asserting that the 'procedure established by law' must be just, fair, and reasonable, not arbitrary.
